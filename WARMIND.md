# Warmind

## Destiny Intelligence Engine

Warmind is the product identity for this project.

### Core promise
Warmind analyzes a player's real Destiny 2 vault and tells them what to run, what to fix, and what to chase.

### Product principles
- Vault-aware, not theoretical
- Activity-aware, not generic
- Stat-aware, not one-stat greedy
- Practical, not spreadsheet bait
- Actionable, not just descriptive

### Current output pillars
- Owned Meta
- Missing Meta
- Farm Next
- GM Meta
- Champion Coverage
- Avoid This
- DPS
- Replacement
- Stat Optimization
- Build Recommendation
- recommendation_export.json

### Roadmap
1. Intelligence engine correctness
2. Stat optimization
3. DIM export
4. Direct equip later
5. GUI wrapper
